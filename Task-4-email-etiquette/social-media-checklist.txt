# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts for College Students

## Do’s
- Think carefully before posting anything publicly
- Keep your profiles professional and respectful
- Use strong passwords and privacy settings
- Verify information before sharing it
- Use social media for networking, learning, and collaboration

## Don’ts
- Do not post offensive, abusive, or harmful content
- Do not overshare private or personal information
- Do not engage in cyberbullying or unnecessary online arguments
- Do not click suspicious links from unknown sources
- Do not share passwords, OTPs, or financial details online
