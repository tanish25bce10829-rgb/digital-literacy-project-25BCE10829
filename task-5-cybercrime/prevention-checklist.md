--Stay Safe Online – Checklist
Never share your OTP, UPI PIN, or bank details with anyone
Do not approve unknown payment requests on UPI apps
Always verify links and avoid clicking suspicious messages
Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts
Enable two-factor authentication wherever possible
Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions
Install apps only from trusted sources like Play Store
Regularly update your phone and apps for security
--UPI / Financial Safety Tips:
Remember: You receive money without entering PIN, but sending requires PIN
Always check the receiver’s name before confirming payment
--Report Cybercrime
National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
